A personal trainer is a fitness professional who helps clients exercise effectively and safely to achieve their fitness goals. Personal trainers are employed by gyms, fitness centres, individuals or corporate clients.

Week 3 Advice - REST PROPERLY

You need to work hard, but you also need to rest properly. our workout may create a positive improvement in a quality such as endurance or strength, but to allow for its development, adequate recovery time must be given. Additionally, how do you maximize your efforts in multiple training components? After a vicious leg day in the weight room, will you be recovered adequately for a quality conditioning day? And what about sport practice? If you’re still hung over from the effects of a previous workout, don’t expect to be a hundred percent. Train hard, but rest hard. It’s better to be over- rather than under-recovered.

Week 2 Advice - TOO LIGHT

You're not lifting heavy enough. “Hard” means you worked each bout to the limit. That means if your goal is a hard set of eight reps, it should be difficult to obtain those eight reps. If you could have performed twelve reps, what exactly did you accomplish? Assure each set is as demarding as possible to complete

Week 1 Advice - FOCUS

You need to focus more on your goal, To optimally expunge body fat, activities that are higher in intensity and physical demand should be employed. This includes intense circuit training, strength training, and interval training. Regarding food intake, a sensible diet incorporating the right balance of good carbohydrates, lean proteins, and healthy fats should be used and in the proper amounts that create a caloric deficit.
