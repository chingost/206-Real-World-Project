About Us

FlexWear

FlexWear offers the finest in fitness oriented clothing, providing insulation, durabilty and comfort. By seamlessly combining anti-static, sweat wicking technology with precision sensors, we have engineered a package designed to give you the tools to elevate your workout. FlexWear apparel is made with natural, lightweight, breathable material that effortlessy adapts to your physique to guarantee an exceptional fit.

The Technology behind FlexSense

FlexSense utilises a set of advanced Electromyography (EMG) and Electrocardiography (ECG) sensors to accurately record and analyse your heart rate and muscle activity. This biometric data can be viewed in real-time via your smartphone, arming you with the knowledge to maximise each rep. FlexSense is strategically placed in key muscle groups around the body and quickly pairs with your mobile device via Bluetooth connectivity. At Flex, we have pushed the boundaries of modern manufacturing techniques to reduce the size of these sensors, ensuring mobility without comprising style and discretion. The data from each session will be fed into your own personal profile where you can track your improvement and recieve helpful feedback from one of our registered personal trainers.

Flex goes beyond the average fitness tracker, giving you an unparalleled insight into your physiology and the opportunity to reach your full potential.
